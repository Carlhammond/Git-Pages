How is this calculated?

The reading is converted to an absolute water density, temperature-corrected using the density of pure water at your instrument's calibration temperature, then salinity is solved from the EOS-80 (UNESCO 1981) seawater equation of state — the same model oceanographers use. Specific gravity is reported on the 25 °C/25 °C basis used throughout the reef hobby (35 ppt = SG 1.0264). Conductivity is an approximation valid near natural-seawater strength.
